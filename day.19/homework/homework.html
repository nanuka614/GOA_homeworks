Day 1: Arrival in Tbilisi

Welcome to Georgia! On the first day of our tour, you will arrive in Tbilisi, the capital city of Georgia.

  • Visit the Holy Trinity Cathedral
  • Take a tour of the Narikala Fortress
  • Explore the Old Town


Day 1 Highlights:

  1. Arrival at Tbilisi International Airport
  2. Check-in at the hotel
  3. Dinner in a traditional Georgian restaurant

Day 2: Discovering the Wonders of Georgia

Today we will explore more of Georgia's beauty and rich culture.

  • Visit the ancient city of Mtskheta
  • Take a cable car ride up to the Tbilisi TV Tower
  • Explore local markets for souvenirs


Day 2 Highlights:

  1. Visit Svetitskhoveli Cathedral
  2. Lunch at a local restaurant in Mtskheta
  3. Wine tasting at a local vineyard

Day 3: Nature & Adventure

On the third day, we will venture out into the scenic countryside of Georgia.

  • Explore the stunning Caucasus Mountains
  • Hike in the Kazbegi National Park
  • Enjoy breathtaking views of Mount Kazbek


Day 3 Highlights:

  1. Visit Gergeti Trinity Church
  2. Have a picnic lunch by the river
  3. Take a scenic drive to Gudauri

Day 4: Departure and Last Moments in Tbilisi

On the final day of the tour, we'll have a relaxing time in Tbilisi before your departure.

  • Stroll along the Rustaveli Avenue
  • Visit the Georgian National Museum
  • Shopping for last-minute souvenirs


Day 4 Highlights:

  1. Final shopping and leisure time
  2. Head to the airport for your flight
