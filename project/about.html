About Marco Polo!

As the first and historic hotel in Gudauri Ski Resort, located at an altitude of 2,003 meters above sea level, just 135 km from Tbilisi International Airport, Hotel Marco Polo has been providing visitors with an unforgettable stay and a ski-in, ski-out experience since 1988.

If you need to relax, the Marco Polo Spa and Wellness Center is the perfect solution, with saunas, as well as indoor and outdoor pools, providing a retreat at any time of the day. To savor the rich flavors of traditional Georgian and European cuisine, visit our signature restaurant, Soliko. Come and experience the warmth of Georgian hospitality in the heart of Gudauri
